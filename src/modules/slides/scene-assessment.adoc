<<<

// tag::slide-2[]
Scan the scene.::

[%step]
--
* Halt! Do not rush in.
* Survey the area:
** Is the _mechanism of injury_ persistent?
** How many people are injured?
** What do you anticipate?
--
// end::slide-2[]

<<<

// tag::slide-3[]
Take precautions.::

[%step]
--
* Consider repositioning.
* Put on protective gear.
** body substance isolation
** physical protection
--
// end::slide-3[]

<<<

// tag::slide-4[]
Organize help.::

[%step]
--
* Check in with your team.
** Who will perform which roles?
* Call for backup.
** Other medics?
** EMS?
** Bystanders?
* Organize the crowd.
--
// end::slide-4[]

<<<

// tag::slide-5[]
Proceed with caution.::

[%step]
--
* Medics do not run.
* Medics walk swiftly with purpose.
* Sometimes you have to walk swiftly _away_.
* Stay calm and confident.
--
// end::slide-5[]

<<<

// tag::slide-6[]
More scene safety basics::

* Never make a new patient!
* Continually reassess the scene for safety.
* Be prepared to move or protect your patient.
// end::slide-6[]
